Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app protect my phone?

The app keeps a constant watch over your device, detecting threats like malware, risky network connections, and system weaknesses. It safeguards against unauthorized access, notifies you of unusual activity, and offers straightforward tips to ensure your phone stays safe, stable, and performs at its best.

Is the app safe to use?

Yes. The app is built with a focus on security and privacy. It follows proven industry standards to safeguard your data, never touches personal information without your approval, and functions transparently to provide a safe and dependable experience.

Do I need a subscription to use the app?

The app provides a mix of free and premium features. While the essential tools are available at no charge, subscribing unlocks advanced functionalities and extra benefits for improved performance and stronger protection.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can stop your subscription anytime via your device’s app store settings. After cancellation, your subscription benefits will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, reach out to your device’s app store support (e.g., Apple App Store) and follow their refund procedures. All refunds are processed directly by the app store in accordance with their policies.
