Privacy Policy for CleanGo

CleanGo is designed primarily as a device utility. Most operations occur directly on your iPhone and do not require transferring your personal information to our servers. Our philosophy is simple: your personal data should stay on your device whenever possible.

By downloading, installing, or using CleanGo, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the practices described below.

1. About CleanGo

CleanGo is a productivity and optimization tool created to help users maintain a clean, organized, and efficient device. Over time, smartphones accumulate duplicate contacts, empty records, outdated calendar events, and unnecessary files that consume storage space and reduce performance. CleanGo provides tools to safely analyze and manage this information.

The Service may include, but is not limited to, the following features:

  • Storage analysis and memory optimization
  • Identification and removal of unnecessary or temporary files
  • Detection and deletion of outdated calendar events
  • Merging duplicate contacts into a single record
  • Removing empty or incomplete contacts
  • Subscription-based premium tools
  • A three-day free trial for new users

2. Our Privacy Principles

We designed CleanGo according to the following principles:

  • Data minimization – we collect only what is strictly necessary
  • Local processing – personal data stays on your device whenever possible
  • No selling of personal information
  • Transparency about permissions and usage
  • Strong security practices

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

You may voluntarily provide certain information when interacting with us, such as when contacting customer support, sending feedback, or subscribing to updates. This may include:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Support messages and attachments
  • Feedback or survey responses

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

To ensure reliability and improve the quality of our Service, we may automatically collect limited technical information that does not directly identify you.

  • Device model and hardware information
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Language and region settings
  • Anonymous identifiers
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • General usage statistics

3.3 Contacts, Calendar, and Storage Data

CleanGo may request access to your Contacts, Calendar, and storage strictly to perform the cleaning functions you explicitly initiate. These permissions allow the app to scan for duplicates, empty entries, or outdated events.

Important: This information is processed locally on your device only. We do not upload, transmit, copy, or store your contacts or calendar content on our servers.

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information for legitimate operational purposes, including:

  • Providing core app functionality
  • Executing cleaning and optimization features requested by you
  • Managing subscriptions and purchases
  • Improving reliability and performance
  • Analyzing bugs and crashes
  • Responding to support inquiries
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not use your information for behavioral advertising or cross‑app tracking.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users located in the European Economic Area or similar jurisdictions, we rely on the following legal bases: consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and compliance with legal obligations.

6. Subscriptions, Free Trial, and Payments

CleanGo offers optional premium subscriptions that may renew automatically. New users may be eligible for a three-day free trial. Billing and payment processing are handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive or store your credit card number, banking details, or other financial information. All payment information is processed securely by Apple according to their own privacy and security standards.

7. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. Measures may include encryption in transit, restricted access controls, and secure development practices.

Although we strive to protect your information, no digital system can be completely secure. You use the Service at your own risk.

8. Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell personal information. We may share limited data only in the following situations:

  • With service providers that help operate the app (analytics, crash reporting, hosting)
  • With Apple for subscription processing
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or prevent fraud

9. Analytics and Diagnostics

We may use third‑party analytics services to understand how users interact with CleanGo. These services collect aggregated and anonymized information only. They do not have access to your contacts, calendar entries, or personal content.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability and the right to withdraw consent.

Because most information is stored locally on your device, deleting the app will typically remove the majority of your data.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal requirements.

12. Children's Privacy

CleanGo is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is discovered, we will delete it promptly.

13. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. When this occurs, we implement appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains protected.

14. App Permissions Explained

  • Contacts: detect duplicates, merge entries, remove empty contacts
  • Calendar: view and clean unnecessary events
  • Storage: analyze files and optimize device memory

15. Third-Party Links

Our website or Service may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage reviewing their policies separately.

16. Do Not Track

CleanGo does not engage in cross‑site tracking or targeted advertising and does not respond to Do Not Track signals because such tracking is not performed.

17. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes indicates acceptance.

18. Your Consent

By using CleanGo, you confirm that you understand and agree to this Privacy Policy.
